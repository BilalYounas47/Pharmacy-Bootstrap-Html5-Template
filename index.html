About Us

We are Professional in Pharmaceutical Care

Grace Pharmacy is a family-run independent community pharmacy since August 2020. We are situated in the Islamabad Capital territory of Pakistan. The Pharmacy is in F-10/3 Markaz. We provide healthcare services within drugstore and the surrounding area, in conjunction with GPs, hospitals and other healthcare providers. We also sell over the counter health and beauty products, including many ranges not available in the larger high street chains. Grace Pharmacy has been owned by Dr Aziz-ur-Rehman S/o Usman Muhammad a business man since 2020. As a family business we pride ourselves on being at the heart of the community and providing help and support to our customers in all their pharmaceutical needs.
